About us

SHRI RAM MURTI SMARAK TRUST

Shri Ram Murti Smarak Trust, a public charitable Trust (Reg.) was established in the year 1990, to commemorate & Cherish memory of veteran freedom fighter, true Gandhian, ex-parliamentarian, ex-minister U.P., Late Ram Murti Ji. The primary objective of the Trust is to provide and promote education and research in the field of Engineering & Technology, Medicine, Humanities, Science and Management.

THE AIM OF SRMS TRUST IS THE UPLIFTMENT OF THE MASSES IRRESPECTIVE OF COLOR, CASTE & CREED.

  • by promoting social values- Social Services
  • by promoting technical and professional education
  • by promoting Health & Medical Education



SOCIAL AND CHARITABLE ACTIVITIES OF THE TRUST

  • Scholarship worth Rs. 3 crore to the meritorious students.
  • All India Talent Scholarship of Rs. 50 Lakh to 50 talented students in Premier Institutes of India.
  • Debate & Story Writing Since 1990
  • Recognition of Eminent personalities with the Ram Murti Pratibha Alankaran Award & Rs. One lakh in cash.
  • Free Vocational Training for the under privileged youth in X-Ray, ECG Technician, Computer Hardware & Software, Machine Fitting, EPABX, Plumbering and Electricians.
  • Free Medical Services
  • SRMS Hospital On Wheel for door to door Screening & Treatment of Blindness, Deafness, Diabetics, T.B., Malnutrition, Hypertension, Cancer & support services for Healthy Mother & Healthy Baby.
  • SRMS Janhit Chikitsa Yojna for free treatment of six patients per day.
  • Free Pharmacy
  • Cataract Eradication Program
  • Govt. Health Programmes
  • Rural Health Camps
  • Free 400 Beds in SRMS Hospital
  • SRMS Samudayik Swastha Yojna at Rs. 365/- per year for treatment upto Rs. 50,000/- for the couple.
